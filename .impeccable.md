## Design Context

### Users
Solo quantitative traders — individuals who write, test, and run trading strategies on their own. They use the app at home on desktop/laptop screens, often for extended sessions. They are technically proficient (comfortable writing Python), value clarity over flash, and want to understand *why* a strategy works or fails, not just *that* it does.

### Brand Personality
- **Alive**: The interface should feel like a living ecosystem, not a dead dashboard. Strategies breathe, age, and evolve.
- **Precise**: Data and metrics must feel exact and trustworthy — this is still a quantitative tool.
- **Warm**: Organic and approachable, not clinical or sterile. Nature-inspired, not medical.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light mode base (preserving existing codebase) with an organic/ecological visual language for the QuantEvo features
- **Visual tone**: Bio-laboratory meets modern data viz — like observing organisms under a microscope. Clean, alive, purposeful.
- **Colors**: Existing light base (#f8fafc) retained. QuantEvo accent shifts from cold indigo toward **living green** tones (#16a34a, #15803d oklch) with warm amber/earth accents (#d97706, #b45309). Health states use natural gradients: verdant green (healthy) → amber (aging) → dried-leaf brown (dying).
- **Typography**: Keep existing Chinese font stack (PingFang SC, Noto Sans SC). No change needed.
- **Anti-references**: No medical/healthcare aesthetics (no heartbeats, no cross symbols, no clinic white). No Japanese dense information overload UI. No traditional dark trading terminal look. No glassmorphism or neon glows.

### Design Principles
1. **Strategies are organisms** — every strategy has a birth, life, and death. The UI should make this metaphor tangible without being cheesy.
2. **Data first, decoration second** — ecological metaphors serve the data, not distract from it.
3. **Clarity over density for new features** — QuantEvo is novel; users need space to understand gene maps and metabolic charts.
4. **Progressive revelation** — show summary health at a glance, let users drill into DNA details.
5. **Respect existing patterns** — new QuantEvo pages should feel like native extensions, not a different product.
